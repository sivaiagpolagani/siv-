Cloud migration is gaining popularity as the benefits outweigh the concerns by a huge extent. The adoption rate has been exponential with businesses ranging from start-ups to big enterprises, increasingly looking at cloud migration as a strategic step. Hosting applications and data on Cloud gives enterprises an edge in terms of increased business agility and effective IT environments. Key benefits include scalability, cost control, mobility, disaster recovery, faster implementation among others.

At Excellent Contact we understand the key challenges that customers face as they go through this journey. To alleviate some of their roadblocks Indium can:

  • Identifying the right infrastructure requirements for a customer to leverage their data and helping in its setup, helping convert CAPEX into OPEX.
  • Identifying the customer’s business objectives and working backwards to identify the right data points to be leveraged to meet them.
  • Setup the appropriate Extract Transform & Load (ETL) processes to get the data points from their source systems into the data platform on the cloud through the right cleansing and transformation steps.
  • Provisioning of real-time, near-real-time and batch processes to make the data available at the right time.
  • Leveraging the data to configure and deploy Data Visualization reports and dashboards for effective BI.
  • Implementation and integration of machine learning, AI and other predictive models using this data to keep businesses one step ahead.
  • Helping customers build, deploy and maintain enterprise web and mobile applications on the cloud, thereby reducing their CAPEX.

Overview

The Excellent Contact Managed Cloud is an add-on service whereby we manage your cloud infrastructure for you, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of cloud computing without having to actively maintain a relatively complex computing system.

The purpose of the Managed Cloud service is to save you time and effort by taking on many of the tasks that you or your IT team would otherwise have perform. Our cloud experts are highly trained and manage cloud infrastructure on a full-time basis, so you can trust Excellent Contact to deliver efficient, quick and reliable service, leaving you to focus on what you do best.

Excellent Contact’s Managed Cloud provides companies with tailor-made solutions consisting of designing, building and maintaining their cloud computing infrastructure. This can range from a single server to thousands of servers, depending on your individual needs

Excellent Contact maintains, supports and monitors your servers from our Network Operation Center on a 24/7 basis. This includes your cloud servers, operating system, databases, web servers, email servers, load balancers, firewalls, and all other components your applications rely on to provide maximum uptime and availability, at all times.

Cut IT costs. Boost IT value.

Imagine the end of expensive file servers, VPNs, legacy hardware, inefficient backups, disaster recovery services and costly IT maintenance. Get a single access point for the most efficient file management, so you can unify fragmented storage, decrease risk exposure, and allow IT to focus on higher-value work.

Simplify content infrastructure.

Retire on-premises file servers once and for all, without sacrificing security or control. Excellent Contact offers the same centralized folder structure of your Windows file servers, so you can embrace the cloud seamlessly without retraining your users.

Give IT control.

A single SaaS platform connects all business content and gives IT system-wide reporting and auditing capabilities. Admins can specify user access down to the subfolder level, remediate unauthorized access quickly with real-time security alerts, and eliminate file replication issues across their organization.

Secure business content.

Forget costly backup and disaster recovery services. Excellent Contact’s multiple data backups and intelligent file versioning ensure data is never lost. A centralized content architecture also empowers IT to identify sensitive information (PII), pinpoint system vulnerabilities, and enforce policies across storage.

Boost user productivity.

Collaborate anytime, anywhere with the fastest file access, even when offline—all without VPN. Streamline work for everyone by eliminating file version conflicts with global file locking, and prevent inbox overflow by sending file links instead of email attachments.
